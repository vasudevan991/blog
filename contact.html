FAQ

Can I ask you medical questions through this form?

While I'm happy to discuss general health topics and provide educational information, I cannot provide specific medical advice or diagnose conditions through this contact form. For personal medical concerns, please consult with a healthcare provider in person.

Do you accept guest posts from other medical professionals?

Yes, I'm open to high-quality guest posts from verified medical professionals. Please include your credentials and a brief outline of your proposed topic when reaching out.

How can I verify the medical information on your blog?

All medical content on this blog is based on current medical literature and best practices. However, I always recommend consulting with your healthcare provider for personalized medical advice. References are provided where appropriate.

Do you provide telemedicine consultations?

Currently, I do not offer telemedicine consultations through this blog. For medical consultations, please contact healthcare providers in your area or use established telemedicine platforms.
